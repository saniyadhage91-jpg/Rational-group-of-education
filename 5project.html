EQUIVALENCE AND RECOGNITION

👉

Board of Open Schooling and Skill Education (BOSSE) has been set up by the Government of Sikkim under an Act passed by the State Legislature (Act No. 14 of 2020, whose Gazette Notification was published on 8th October, 2020).

👉

The Association of Indian Universities (AIU), has granted Equivalence to BOSSE, Sikkim and its certificates for admission to higher education.

👉

National Institute of Open Schooling (NIOS), Govt. of India has recognised BOSSE, Sikkim and its certificates.
